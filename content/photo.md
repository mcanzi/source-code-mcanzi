**South Korea & Taiwan** (2025, *3 weeks*). Three weeks down two coastlines. South Korea ran from Seoul to the royal tombs of Gyeongju and the harbours of Busan; Taiwan followed its west coast south, through Taipei, the old quarters of Tainan, and Kaohsiung. Shot on digital and film.
